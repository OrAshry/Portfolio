Who am I?

I am a Computer Science student at the Open University of Israel, driven by a deep-seated curiosity about how complex systems work and the logic that powers our digital world.
My journey into technology began with a focus on efficiency, leading me to develop a bespoke software tool to solve real-world operational challenges.
Beyond my formal studies, I am a true tech enthusiast—I enjoy diving into hacking stories, exploring the intersection of hardware and software, and staying updated on emerging technologies.
This intellectual curiosity, combined with the disciplined self-study required by the Open University, has shaped me into a resourceful and independent problem-solver.
Ultimately, I am motivated by the opportunity to apply my skills within a collaborative team to build meaningful, innovative technology that helps advance our society and makes a genuine difference.
